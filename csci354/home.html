Does Cardio Kill Gains?

Cardio can be a helpful tool when it comes to fat loss when used right.

Essentially, to lose fat you need to burn more calories than you use, which can be done either through eating in a deficit, or working out to burn exrtra calories, and ofcourse adding cardio to do the same. So if you want to gain muslce and lose fat at the same time you can add preferbaly morning cardio to your routine.

Cardio first thing in the morning has proven to be most beneficial if you want to elevate your metabolism rate and increase fat loss while gaining lean muslce.
