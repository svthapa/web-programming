Upper Body /Lower Body Split

This split focuses on more compound movements and works the whole upper body and lower body in a single day twice a week.

Week A
Day Workout
UPPER
  1. Bench Press 4x8
  2. Overhead Press 4x8
  3. Barbell Row 4x6
  4. Dips 4x10
  5. Barbell Curl 3x10
  6. Shrugs 3x10
LOWER
  1. Squats 5x5
  2. Deadlift 3x6
  3. Leg Press 4x8
  4. Calf Raise 4x10
Week B
Day Workout
UPPER
  1. Inclie Bench Press 4x3
  2. Overhead Dumbell Press 4x8
  3. Dumbell Row 4x8
  4. Decline Barbell Press 4x8
  5. Dumbell Hammer Curl 3x10
  6. Cable Crossover 3x8
  7. Shrugs 3x10
LOWER
  1. Squats 3x8
  2. Deadlift 4x3
  3. Lunges 4x8
  4. Squat Clean 4x5
  5. Calf Raise 4x10
